Executive Summary: Add/Edit Role Form Initiative

Back to Add New Role

This document provides a concise, executive-friendly overview of the Add/Edit Role Form initiative, highlighting key capabilities and their strategic sequencing across phases. It serves as a decision-making tool to understand the scope and sequencing of our talent management platform enhancements.

Role Details - Comprehensive Form (Phase 2)

This phase focuses on enhancing the core Add/Edit Role form to capture richer role metadata and improve usability, building on the foundation established in Phase 1.

  • Multi-step Role Creation Form (9 steps): A guided, intuitive workflow for comprehensive role definition, ensuring data completeness and consistency. This form captures a total of 37 fields across its steps:
    • Step 1: Role Overview: 6 fields
    • Step 2: Organization & Position Structure: 5 fields
    • Step 3: Classification & Hierarchy: 5 fields
    • Step 4: Skills, Capabilities, Competencies & Behaviors: 7 fields
    • Step 5: Performance, Growth & Mobility: 4 fields
    • Step 6: Administrative & Compliance Details: 4 fields
    • Step 7: Custom Fields: 1 dynamic field
    • Step 8: Metadata & Activity Log: 5 read-only fields
    • Step 9: Review & Edit: 0 new input fields (review/action step)
  • Skills Ontology Integration: Seamless integration with our standardized skills framework for consistent skill mapping, leveraging existing organizational knowledge.
  • AI-Assisted Content Generation: Smart suggestions for role descriptions, skills, and other metadata, including the ability to re-generate and review up to 5 versions, accelerating content creation.
  • Tooltips for Field-Level Guidance: Context-aware help text for each field to reduce user errors and improve form completion quality.
  • Draft Saving & Review Workflow: Ability to save incomplete forms as drafts and a consolidated review step for final definitions before submission.
  • Comprehensive Bulk Import/Export (including Export to Excel): Expanded functionality to import and export all role fields via standardized templates, supporting efficient data migration and management.
  • Print Preview Support: Allows users to preview the full role definition in a print-friendly format for review and sharing.
  • User-Level Permissions: Ensures compliance with defined user-level permissions for adding and editing roles, maintaining data security and integrity.
  • Enhanced User Experience: Focus on accessibility (WCAG compliance), mobile responsiveness, and clear error handling to ensure an intuitive and efficient user journey.

Admin Configuration Suite (Phase 2)

This phase introduces the Enterprise Role Form Configuration Suite, a comprehensive backend solution for our SaaS customers. This suite provides administrators with a guided, wizard-like experience to deeply customize every aspect of the "Add/Edit Role Form."

Key capabilities, structured as a sequential wizard, include:

  • Step 1: Global Settings & Feature Toggles: Enable or disable major functionalities like AI assistance, bulk import/export, print preview, and field-level guidance.
  • Step 2: Branding & Appearance: Apply corporate identity to exported documents with custom logos, headers, and footers. (Optional)
  • Step 3: Form Structure & Field Configuration: Granular control over every field in the user-facing form, grouped by their original step, including the ability to define and manage a library of organization-specific custom fields:
    • Role Overview Fields
    • Organization & Position Structure Fields
    • Classification & Hierarchy Fields
    • Skills, Capabilities, Competencies & Behaviors
    • Performance, Growth & Mobility
    • Administrative & Compliance Details
    • Custom Field Management
  • Step 4: Localization & Cultures: A dedicated workspace for translating the form into multiple languages, including RTL support.
  • Step 5: Permissions & Access Control: Define granular, role-based access control (RBAC) for form functionalities.
  • Step 6: Versioning & Deployment: Manage and deploy configuration sets safely with a version history.
  • Step 7: Audit Log: A comprehensive and immutable log of all administrative activities.
  • Step 8: Review & Activate: A final summary step that provides a clear "diff" view before deploying changes.

This evolution is critical to capturing the enterprise market segment, offering unparalleled flexibility and control that allows large organizations to seamlessly integrate our platform into their existing global HR ecosystems.

Role Architecture Requirements (Phase 3)

Phase 3 will leverage the enhanced data captured in Phase 2 to develop and implement a full Role Architecture framework, enabling advanced talent intelligence solutions.

  • Hierarchical role relationships: Defining how roles fit into the broader organizational structure.
  • Career pathing capabilities: Tools to visualize and plan career progression within the organization.
  • Department & job family structures: Establishing clear organizational groupings for roles.
  • Advanced analytics & reporting: Deeper insights into workforce data based on standardized role definitions.
  • Organization design tools: Capabilities to model and manage organizational structures effectively.
