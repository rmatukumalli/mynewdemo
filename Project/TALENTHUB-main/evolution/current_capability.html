Our Current Capability

Percipio delivers a connected learning experience, seamlessly integrating essential components like Home, My Learning, Library, and various specialized tools. These elements work in harmony, guided by a intuitive sidebar navigation, to actively support and accelerate your professional development journey.

Home

Your personalized landing page: a welcoming entry point featuring intelligent search, relevant learning suggestions, progress tracking, benchmarks, and more to kickstart your daily learning.

My Learning (Activity Dashboard)

A comprehensive dashboard to track all your learning activities. View your profile, achievements, assignments, and live learning sessions, providing a holistic view of your progress.

Library

Your gateway to all learning resources. Explore calendars, curated journeys, virtual bookshelf, AI simulations, skill benchmarks, and certification preparation tools to expand your knowledge.

Role Advisor

Discover essential skill requirements and career progression paths tailored to your current role or future aspirations, providing personalized guidance for professional growth.

Certifications

Prepare for and track your professional certifications with dedicated resources. Validate your expertise and enhance your credentials effectively.

Skill Benchmarks

Gauge your proficiency in key skills. Identify strengths and areas for development, receiving personalized content recommendations to bridge any gaps.

Practice Labs

Acquire practical, hands-on experience in real-world sandboxed environments. Apply and hone technical skills such as coding, IT operations, and cybersecurity.

AI Simulations

Engage with interactive AI-driven scenarios to develop complex capabilities. Practice leadership, communication, and decision-making in realistic, risk-free contexts.

AI Assistant

Leverage AI-powered support for instant answers and personalized learning recommendations, significantly enhancing your overall Percipio learning journey.
