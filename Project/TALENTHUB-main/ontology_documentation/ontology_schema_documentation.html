Application-Table Linkages

This section explains how different functional modules of the application interact with the database tables, providing a high-level overview of data usage.

Skills Management

Relevant APIs: skill_api.py, proficiency_api.py, skilltag_api.py, skill_relationship_api.py, user_skill_api.py, skill_gap_api.py

Primary Tables: skill, proficiency, skill_tag, skill_relationship, user_skill, skill_gap

Explanation: This module handles the creation, retrieval, updating, and deletion of skills, their proficiency levels, tags, and relationships between skills. It also manages user-specific skills and identifies skill gaps, forming the core of the skills ontology.

Job Architecture

Relevant APIs: job_architecture.py, organization_api.py, business_unit_api.py, department_api.py, rolegroup_api.py, joblevel_api.py, jobprofile_api.py, role_api.py

Primary Tables: organization, business_unit, department, role_group, job_level, job_profile, role

Explanation: This module defines the organizational structure, job roles, levels, and profiles. It links skills and competencies to specific roles and job requirements, providing the framework for talent management.

Competency & Capability Management

Relevant APIs: capability_api.py, competency_api.py, behavior_api.py

Primary Tables: capability, competency, behavior

Explanation: Manages the definition and relationships of capabilities, competencies, and associated behaviors within the organization, providing a hierarchical structure for defining required attributes.

User & Career Management

Relevant APIs: user_api.py, user_career_history_api.py, career_path_api.py

Primary Tables: user, user_career_history, career_path

Explanation: Handles user profiles, their career progression, and historical roles within the organization, enabling personalized career development and internal mobility.

Learning & Development

Relevant APIs: learning_resource_api.py, learning_path_api.py, learning_path_resource_association_api.py, assessment_api.py, certification_api.py

Primary Tables: learning_resource, learning_path, learning_path_resources, assessment, certification

Explanation: Manages learning content, defines structured learning paths, tracks assessments for skill validation, and records user certifications, supporting continuous learning and development.

Feedback & Projects

Relevant APIs: feedback_api.py, project_or_assignment_api.py

Primary Tables: feedback, project_or_assignment

Explanation: Manages feedback mechanisms for performance and development, and tracks projects or assignments, linking them to users, roles, and organizations for comprehensive talent insights.
