Welcome to Your Career Navigator!

This page is your personal guide to understanding and planning your career journey within Akara using the Career Navigator. We're here to help you visualize your growth and identify exciting opportunities.

How to Use This Page:

  • Review Your Current Role: See details about your current position and skills.
  • Explore Aspirational Roles: Discover potential next steps in your career using the navigator. Click on any role to see more details.
  • Analyze Path Details: Understand the skills you have and those you can develop for a chosen aspirational role.
  • Plan Your Development: Use the "Learning Focus" to identify key skills for growth and access development resources.
  • Discover More: Check out "Explore Further Opportunities" for additional role suggestions tailored to you.

We're excited to support your growth and development at Akara!
